GirlsBandCry

"Girls Band Cry" (ガールズバンドクライ Gāruzu Bando Kurai) is an anime and multimedia project by Toei Animation about a group of girls with circumstances forming a band. It premiered from April 6th to June 29th, 2024 on Japanese stations Tokyo MX, Sun TV, KBS Kyoto, and BS11. The show was announced in March 2023. That was followed up with the release of several music videos over the following year. The production is headed up by Love Live! Sunshine director Kazuo Sakai and that show's writer Jukki Hanada.
Through June and July of 2021 Toei accepted applications for a talent search called Girl's Rock Audition. They held auditions through the remainder of the year. The members of Togenashi Togeari are portrayed by five individuals selected from these auditions.

Main Band

Togenashi Togeagari

Togenashi Togeari (トゲナシトゲアリ) is an all-girl band in Girls Band Cry. The band consists of five members named Nina Iseri on vocals, Momoka Kawaragi on guitar, Subaru Awa on drums, Tomo Ebizuka on keyboard, and Rupa on bass

Through June and July of 2021 Toei accepted applications for a talent search called Girl's Rock Audition. They held auditions through the remainder of the year. The members of Togenashi Togeari are portrayed by five individuals selected from these auditions.

Members

Nina

Name: Nina Iseri

Role: Vocalist

Birth: October 24

Height: 152cm

Gender: Female

Description: Nina comes from a small country town where she had a strict family upbringing. Due to her coming from a country town, she is often referred to as a "country bumpkin" and related terms from Momoka Kawaragi. She is also a high school drop-out.

Momoka

Name: Momoka Kawaragi

Role: Guitarist, Leader

Birth: December 9th

Height: 164cm

Gender: Female

Description: Momoka is a 20-year-old street musician with a straightforward and tomboyish personality. She dislikes feminine attire and opts for plain and simple hairstyles and clothing. She has a cheerful and caring nature, exuding the aura of a reliable senior member in a sports team. In the past she was a member of the band Diamond Dust, but later left the band.

Subaru

Name: Subaru Awa

Role: Drumer

Birth: April 27

Height: 158cm

Gender: Female

Description: Subaru is a girl who attends a performing arts school. She is considered a promising talent and has appeared in commercials. Mirei is a socially adept and charming beauty. She takes pride in her stunning appearance, which could easily be mistaken for that of a model. However, beneath her exterior, she possesses a strong-willed and competitive nature, characteristic of a proud young lady. She is the granddaughter of the very successful actress Tendo Awa.

Rupa

Name: Tomo Ebizuka

Role: Pianist

Birth: March 22

Height: 148cm

Gender: Female

Description: Originally hailing from a well-known affluent family in her hometown, Tomo is now living in an apartment through room-sharing. She maintains a cold and aloof attitude towards the world. Much like a hedgehog, she is highly wary and doesn't easily open up to others.

Rupa

Name: Rupa

Role: Bassist

Birth: June 28

Height: 169cm

Gender: Female

Description: Rupa is a girl born to a South Asian father and a Japanese mother. She exudes artistic sensibility and possesses sharp intellect. Considered a genius due to her calm way of speaking and modest attitude, she earns the respect of others. However, her flaw lies in being a heavy drinker and having an excessively strong character. In episode 7 it is revealed to us that her family died in a car accident.
